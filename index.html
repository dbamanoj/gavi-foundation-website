About Gavi Foundation and Trust

A registered charitable trust committed to supporting underprivileged students and rural communities across Karnataka.

know more →

We are pleased to introduce Gavi Foundation and Trust, a registered charitable trust established on 13th August 2025, with a long-standing commitment of over 16 years toward uplifting underprivileged students and rural communities across Karnataka.

Our foundation supports children from 1st Standard to 10th Standard, primarily in rural and economically disadvantaged areas. With the expansion of our outreach to more villages and schools across Karnataka, we have officially registered the trust to strengthen transparency, accountability, and scale.

Vision & Mission

Our purpose is to uplift students, strengthen communities, and protect the environment.

🌟 Vision

To build a society where every child has access to quality education, every individual enjoys basic health and well-being, and communities thrive in harmony with nature.

🎯 Mission

To support underprivileged children by providing essential educational resources and opportunities for learning and growth.

To promote basic health, wellness, and preventive care among rural and underserved communities.

To encourage environmental responsibility and conservation practices that protect natural resources for future generations.

To collaborate with local communities, volunteers, and partners to drive sustainable and meaningful social impact.
